## How to Submit a Grievance

Send your grievance by email to **001henrycreel@gmail.com** with the subject line:

```
Grievance: [brief description] — [your store URL or order reference if applicable]
```

**Your grievance should include:**

1. Your full name
2. Your contact email address
3. A clear description of your complaint
4. The relevant Shoprift job ID, store URL, or order reference (if applicable)
5. Any supporting documents or screenshots

## What We Handle

The Grievance Officer handles complaints regarding:

- **Data and Privacy** — requests for data access, correction, or deletion under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023
- **Content** — complaints about extracted content, intellectual property concerns, or wrongful data extraction
- **Service** — dissatisfaction with extraction results, delivery issues, or billing disputes
- **Legal notices** — requests to cease and desist, legal notices, or regulatory enquiries

---

## Escalation

If your grievance is not resolved to your satisfaction within 15 days, you may:

1. **Data Protection Board of India** — once the Board is operational under the DPDP Act 2023, personal data complaints may be escalated there.

2. **Consumer Forum** — disputes relating to the Service may be escalated to the District Consumer Disputes Redressal Commission under the Consumer Protection Act 2019.

3. **Arbitration** — disputes covered by the Terms of Service arbitration clause may be referred to arbitration in Delhi as specified in the Terms of Service.

---

## Reporting Illegal Content

If you believe Shoprift's platform or any content delivered through it violates Indian law, you may report it to the Grievance Officer using the contact above. We will act on valid complaints expeditiously and in accordance with our obligations under the IT Act 2000 and IT Rules 2021.
