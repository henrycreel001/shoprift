## 1. Definitions

**1.1** "**Agreement**" means this Migration Consent & Authorisation Agreement, including all schedules.

**1.2** "**Platform**" means the dm2buy store accessible at the URL provided by the Seller during onboarding, including all product listings, images, descriptions, pricing, collection structure, and store metadata associated with that URL.

**1.3** "**Shoprift**" means the migration service operated by Mayank Malik (contact: 001henrycreel@gmail.com).

**1.4** "**Seller**" means the individual or entity accepting this Agreement and initiating the migration.

**1.5** "**Content**" means all data extracted from the Platform under this Agreement, including product names, descriptions, pricing, images, category structure, and store metadata. It does not include customer personal data or order history unless the Seller expressly instructs extraction of such data and confirms they hold the legal right to transfer it.

**1.6** "**Extraction**" means the automated retrieval of Content from the Platform using Shoprift's tooling.

**1.7** "**Delivery Package**" means the structured output produced by Shoprift, comprising JSON data, CSV files, and downloaded image files, delivered to the Seller.

## 3. Authorisation to Act as Agent

**3.1** The Seller irrevocably authorises Shoprift to access the Platform URL provided by the Seller and to retrieve all Content from that URL using automated tools, for the sole and limited purpose of producing the Delivery Package.

**3.2** This authorisation is specific to:
- (a) the Platform URL provided at the time of this Agreement;
- (b) the purpose of producing the Delivery Package for the Seller's own use; and
- (c) the single extraction job initiated under this Agreement.

**3.3** Shoprift does not acquire any ownership over the Content. Shoprift's access is strictly as agent of the Seller. All right, title, and interest in the Content remains with the Seller (subject to any rights retained by dm2buy or third parties under their own agreements with the Seller).

**3.4** This authorisation terminates automatically upon delivery of the Delivery Package or, if extraction fails, upon written notice from either party.

## 4. Shoprift's Obligations

**4.1** Shoprift will use commercially reasonable efforts to extract the Content from the Platform and produce the Delivery Package.

**4.2** Shoprift will not use the Content for any purpose other than producing and delivering the Delivery Package to the Seller.

**4.3** Shoprift will delete its working copies of the Content within 30 days of delivery, unless a longer retention period is required by law or agreed in writing with the Seller.

**4.4** Shoprift will maintain appropriate technical safeguards to protect the Content during Extraction and storage.
